Controls
--------
According to the Toradora VN:

* __Cross__ - Hide Text
* __Circle__ - Continue
* __Square__ - Menu
* __Triangle__ - Backlog
* __L__ - Nothing
* __R__ - Skip

I should probably offer a choice between Japanese and Western (my own) controls:

* __Cross__ - Continue
* __Circle__ - Hide Text
* __Square__ - Backlog
